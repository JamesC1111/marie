If practical uncertainty is making it harder to start, it can help to focus on a few clear questions.

Useful questions may include:

- How long is a session?
- What is the first session like?
- How are confidentiality and its limits explained?
- Do I need to decide everything before booking?
- How do cancellations work?

You can read about many of these on the [FAQ page](/faq) or [How counselling works](/how-counselling-works), and you can also ask them directly when you make contact.
