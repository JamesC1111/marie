## What this can feel like

You may feel tense, restless, or stuck in overthinking. It can become hard to switch off, sleep well, or stay present during the day.

## How counselling may help

Counselling can help you slow things down, understand pressure points, and build approaches that feel realistic in your own life.

## What we might work on together

- Recognising stress triggers and early warning signs.
- Grounding and calming strategies for difficult moments.
- Boundaries around work, family, and expectations.
- Confidence in handling uncertainty.

## How to start

A short initial chat can help us decide the best next step for you.
