## What this can feel like

Grief can affect sleep, mood, concentration, and everyday routines. Some days may feel numb, while others feel intense or unpredictable.

## How counselling may help

Counselling gives you space to speak openly about your loss, your memories, and your worries, without pressure to feel a certain way.

## What we might work on together

- Making room for grief in daily life.
- Coping with difficult dates and reminders.
- Managing strain on work and relationships.
- Finding steady routines during a difficult period.
