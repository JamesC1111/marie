## What this can feel like

You may be carrying fear, shame, anger, numbness, or confusion. It can be hard to trust others or to feel safe in your day-to-day life.

## How counselling may help

Counselling provides a supportive, steady space where you can choose what to share and when. There is no pressure to discuss anything before you are ready.

## What we might work on together

- Building safety and stability first.
- Grounding techniques for difficult moments.
- Supporting boundaries and choice.
- Reconnecting with daily life at your own pace.

If you wish, contact me by phone or email to ask about availability.
