## What this can feel like

Young people can struggle with school pressure, social stress, low confidence, bullying, and changes at home.

## How counselling may help

Counselling offers a calm space for young people to feel heard, build coping skills, and strengthen communication with trusted adults.

## What we might work on together

- Coping with stress, worry, and confidence concerns.
- Support around school, peer, or family pressures.
- Building practical emotional regulation skills.
- Planning supportive next steps with parents or guardians where appropriate.
