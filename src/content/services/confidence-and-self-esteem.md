## What this can feel like

You might be hard on yourself, avoid situations, or feel "not good enough" even when trying your best.

## How counselling may help

Counselling can help you understand where these patterns came from and build a kinder, more realistic view of yourself.

## What we might work on together

- Noticing self-critical patterns.
- Building confidence in decisions and boundaries.
- Strengthening self-respect in relationships and work.
- Developing practical habits that support progress.
