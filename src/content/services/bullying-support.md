## What this can feel like

Bullying can affect confidence, concentration, sleep, and trust. It can leave you feeling anxious, isolated, or constantly on alert.

## How counselling may help

Counselling can provide a safe place to process what happened, reduce isolation, and plan practical next steps.

## What we might work on together

- Making sense of your experience.
- Rebuilding confidence after repeated stress.
- Planning boundaries and supports.
- Preparing for difficult conversations where needed.

Please contact me by phone or email if you would like to discuss support.
