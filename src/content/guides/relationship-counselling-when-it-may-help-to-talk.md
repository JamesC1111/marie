Relationship strain can build quietly. Sometimes the problem is obvious. Sometimes it is a sense that communication has become tense, distant, or difficult to repair.

## Signs it may help to talk

Support may help when:

- the same arguments repeat without resolution
- communication feels tense or shut down
- family pressures are affecting trust or closeness
- you feel worn down and unsure how to change the pattern

## Counselling is not about taking sides

A counselling space should make it easier to understand the pattern, the pressure, and what may need attention. It is not about forcing a neat answer or pretending everything can be solved quickly.
